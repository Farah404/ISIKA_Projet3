What is the future of web development?


Along with the advancements of technology, the future of web development tends to change every single year. It is presented when the number of websites all over the world has increased dramatically. In addition, businesses nowadays depend more on online purchases via apps or websites rather than conventional stores. Therefore, to compete with such a competitive digital market, your website must become more potent. To do this, you must create innovative ideas and identify the newest web development trends. In this article, we will give a prediction about the future of web development. Check them now!



Artificial Intelligence (AI)


In the coming days, it is presumable that the role of artificial intelligence will become wider than ever in the world of websites. It can not be denied that AI will be the future of web development as this phase has developed at a higher speed. Currently, many leading companies have launched AI-specific. In addition, Gird is one of the most sought-after AI implementations that permits content-specific website development in no time. Furthermore, the launch of ADI tools has allowed automated website development which can be customized further according to the web developers. Two common examples of AI systems are Apple’s Siri and Google’s Alexa have features of recognizing human speech, analyzing it by tracking patterns, and offering a better user experience thanks to information interpreted. One another example of the fastest-growing AI trends is speech recognition. It plays an essential role and an indispensable part in revolutionizing the mobile and website development world. Gradually, big names such as Facebook, Microsoft, Google,… are investing a large amount of money in AI. Therefore, web developers should include features and functionality developed by AI. It will bring radical changes into the domain of web development.


Internet of Things (IoT)


As a survey by Statista, there will be several around 32 billion connected devices in 2021. The huge growth in the number of IoT devices will positively impact the web development process because companies tend to manage these devices via laptop or desktop. In addition, the integration of devices with the website also plays an important role in bringing better services to customers. For example, IoT can be used to analyze fitness bands or smartwatches. They can track all activities of the users like tracking sleep, nutrition, and optimize the health goals by storing the information over the cloud. While only a few websites have an IoT integration currently, the concept will continue to focus on the near future as the number of connected devices will gradually increase.



Virtual Reality


Over the past few years, virtual reality has been one of the most determined elements that have had a positive impact on the future of web development. Web developers have made full use of virtual reality to implement the top traits of technology to bring their users a stimulating environment. The websites equipped with virtual reality can permit the users to enjoy the 3D world in real-time and have engagement with it. Several famous organizations are looking for web developers with adequate skills in virtual reality that can be implemented in their business models. This can offer their clients to engage virtually with their products which can increase general customer satisfaction.

360 Degree Video


One of the most sought-after skills that are found by almost all organizations worldwide is the ability to create a 360-degree video. It can support the development of a website by offering an interactive and enhanced UI design. It brings the users a virtual experience of a product that is easy to describe and explain. If the designers have prior experience in virtual reality, they can have more opportunities to create 360 Degree Video to increase customer engagement.

Code Injection Exposure


The designing concept – Response Animation Idle Load has been here since 2015. However, it is expected to thrive in 2021. This concept brings a faster load time at under 1 second and the better performance of the website. It mainly focuses on giving a satisfactory user experience. An article from Smashing Magazine has revealed that RAIL breaks down UX into tap, scroll, drag…actions and gives performance goals along with a structure that is necessary to give better performance. In summary, the concept of RAIL is really useful for web designers to follow the user-centric method.

Response Animation Idle Load (RAIL)


These days, as many applications offer the users comment and feedback with the support of forms, there are some common ways to add a malware code injection. For example, if the applications do not prevent the user from adding a minimum number of characters into the login form and allow the use of characters, it will be easy for the attacker to add the code into the form for accessing all data of the server.

Single Page Applications (SPAs)


Currently, most websites do not ask to add various web pages and financial content. However, it will make more sense to have a straightforward website. In addition, Single Page Applications (SPAs) will be effective for mobile screens. The visitors can smoothly scroll up and down and get the information that they want to have on a single page. You don’t need to have menus that maybe complicate the browser experience. If you have an eCommerce website, you will probably find that it will be beneficial to design and make loading time faster. SPAs have been an attractive topic since 2020. Currently, more companies are allowing them to balance the expense as well as give fast information to the visitors. Facebook, Gmail, and Github are popular examples of SPAs. A SPA can eliminate the specifications of reloading the website page by loading all the content via JavaScript. SPAs can work inside the browser and present natural behavior. Users tend to like it because SPAs have reduced waiting time and directly rendered the web pages in the browser of the users.



Blockchain


Bitcoins have gained popularity since 2020. However, the story of blockchain technology has just begun. The technology is considered to bring radical changes to the web industry. Although Blockchain is eliminating the necessity of intermediaries, it has offered a safe and secure online transaction. This innovative technology has utilized standard data storage for supporting users to store data in various locations worldwide.
